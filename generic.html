About Me

Hi, I’m a Full Stack Web Developer. I started by leading people before I ever led code — guiding a team of students to better outcomes by building systems that helped them learn more effectively. That instinct to remove friction carried forward when I built a marketplace that made textbooks more affordable by cutting out unnecessary intermediaries. As I moved deeper into software, I began shipping real solutions: collaborating across time zones, delivering freelance projects, and contributing to open-source codebases used by others. I don’t just write code — I identify bottlenecks, design practical solutions, and follow through to delivery. I also share what I learn publicly, reinforcing clarity of thought and strong documentation habits. Today, I bring leadership, product sense, and proven execution to engineering teams that value ownership and impact.

Apart from coding, I enjoy to play chess, experimenting with new technologies, solving complex problems!
Feel free to reach out if you want to collaborate on exciting projects, discuss innovative ideas, or just talk tech!
